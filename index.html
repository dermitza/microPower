    The microPower power supply

    TBA description

    Features:

    The microPower features:

    Power input:

    • VUSB: 5-5.5V
    • VBAT: one-cell LiIon at max 4.2V *

    Power output (Concurrent)

    • Linearly regulated 3.3V, 400mA, 500mA peak
    • Switching regulated 5V, 800mA
    • Unregulated 3.3-5.5V (depending if battery or USB is providing power)

    I/O

    • USB to Serial
    • I2C for sensors

    Sensors (I2C)

    • Battery fuel gauge
    • Battery current sensing

    Components used:

    • FT232RL USB to serial converter (also providing VUSB)
    • MCP73871 Battery charger and load balancer
    • MIC5205 3.3V LDO Regulator
    • MAX17043 Battery fuel gauge
    • ACS711 Hall-effect current sensor
    • MCP3021 10-Bit A/D Converter to I2C

    Revisions & Issues

    rev 0.1

    Rev 0.1 is the first published revision. This revision is missing the following items:

    • Power on/off switch. An on/off switch would be a useful addition instead of adding/removing the battery.
    • Power LED. It would be useful to know when the board is powered and functional.
